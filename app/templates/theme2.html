DISTINGUISHED PERSONALITIES




This walk features several significant and historical locations within the Crawley Campus of the University of Western Australia. Nearly all locations have been named after distinguished UWA personalities who were alive between the late 1800s until the late 1900s. Most people featured on the walk have made significant academic or administrative contributions to the university and are held in high regard by university alumni, staff, students, and the general public.
