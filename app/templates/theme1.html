ARTS THROUGH TIME

A walk showcasing the Arts of UWA as they were built.

This walk takes you through the Arts section of the UWA campus while showing the chronology of the buildings. Most of the locations you are taken to in the order that they were built, taking you in a loop around the north-eastern corner of campus. The walk starts from the Queen and Duke Trees, and takes you up to the Music department and then back down to the University club, allowing you to explore areas of the campus and build up a picture of the campus over the years.
